Design Principles
These principles guide every change to Blobio.
Prefer emergence over scripting.
If a mechanic creates surprising behavior from simple rules, it is
probably a good addition.
Prefer interactions over abilities.
Interesting gameplay should arise from how systems interact, not from
adding more buttons.
The environment is part of the game.
Hazards are not obstacles---they are participants.
Growth changes influence, not options.
Getting larger should change physics, leverage, and momentum rather than
unlocking new powers.
Keep rules understandable.
Complex outcomes should emerge from simple mechanics.
Preserve experimentation.
Blobio is a laboratory first and a game second.
