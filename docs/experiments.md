# Experiments

This document records notable experiments and observations.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Environmental Eliminations

**Question**

Can the environment replace traditional combat?

**Observation**

Removing direct damage shifted the focus toward positioning, leverage,
and momentum.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Temporary Tethers

**Question**

Can relationships become the primary mechanic?

**Observation**

Brief connections created opportunities for cooperation, betrayal,
dragging, and slingshot plays.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Growth as Influence

**Question**

Should growth unlock abilities?

**Decision**

No.

Growth increases mass, momentum, pushing force, and tether influence
while preserving the same rule set.
