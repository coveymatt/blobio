# Design

## Why Blobio Exists

Blobio is an exploration of **emergent gameplay** inspired by Complex
Adaptive Systems (CAS).

Rather than designing dozens of mechanics, Blobio asks a different
question:

> How much interesting behavior can emerge from a handful of simple
> rules?

The project intentionally favors interaction over complexity.

## Core Principles

-   Simple local rules
-   Emergent global behavior
-   Environment over weapons
-   Momentum over damage
-   Relationships over abilities
-   Experimentation over optimization

## The Core Loop

1.  Collect pellets.
2.  Increase mass.
3.  Become more influential in collisions.
4.  Form temporary tethers.
5.  Manipulate opponents into hazards.
6.  Adapt as the system evolves.

No mechanic exists in isolation. Every rule is intended to influence the
behavior of the entire system.
